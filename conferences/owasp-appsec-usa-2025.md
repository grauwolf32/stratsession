# OWASP Global AppSec USA 2025

**Washington, DC · November 3–7, 2025**

OWASP's largest US conference of the year. 800+ attendees. Six tracks: Builder/Developer, Breaker, Defender, Manager/Culture, OWASP Projects, and a dedicated **OWASP Project Showcase** + **Project Demo Room**.

## Tracks & training

Five-day program with Mon–Wed training (threat modeling, AI security and security with AI, privacy and security programs) and Thu–Fri main conference. Hands-on activities included a CTF, Project Demo Room sessions, and Meet-the-Mentor program.

## Why this matters

OWASP USA 2025 is a **convergence point for the open-source AppSec stack** — most enterprise AppSec teams base their tooling on at least three OWASP projects (Top 10, DefectDojo, SAMM, CycloneDX). The 2025 release cycle for these projects coincided with this event:

- **CycloneDX 2.0** signals BOM architecture maturing — the format has stabilized enough to expand into AI/ML BOMs and security-finding interchange.
- **Top 10 (2025)** is the SAST-rule and developer-training pivot point — expect commercial SAST vendors to ship updated rulepacks in Q1 2026.
- **CRS next-gen rule language** addresses a long-running developer-experience pain in WAF rules.
- **SAMM update** sharpens the maturity-model checkpoints program leads use to justify investment.
